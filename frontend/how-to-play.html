How to Play

Game Overview

Mafia Voteout is a social deduction game where players try to find and eliminate the mafia members through discussion and voting, while the mafia tries to eliminate all innocent players.

Roles

Mafia

Hidden members who know each other. During the day, vote with everyone else. At night, secretly eliminate an innocent player.

Villager

Innocent player with no special abilities. Vote during the day to eliminate suspected mafia members.

Game Flow

  1. Day Phase: All players discuss and vote to eliminate someone they think is mafia.
  2. Elimination: The player with the most votes is eliminated. This stage lasts for 2 mins (120s)
  3. Night Phase: Mafia chooses an innocent to eliminate. This stage lasts for 1 minute (60s)
  4. Repeat: Continue until mafia is eliminated or they equal innocents.

Winning Conditions

Town Wins

The mafia is eliminated through voting.

Mafia Wins

Mafia members equal innocent players.

Tips & Strategy

  • Listen carefully to what other players say and look for inconsistencies.
  • Pay attention to voting patterns and alliances.
  • Mafia should create alibis and blend in with innocents.

FAQ

How do you know you are the mafia?

You will see the mafia badge next to your name during the game and the kill button will be active for you.

How to win?

Town (innocents) win when all mafia members are eliminated through voting. Mafia win when the number of mafia equals the number of innocent players.

What are the AI features?

In the voting arena, players can use the AI agent to help find out who the mafia is by typing @idara. Note: The AI suggestion is not always accurate so don't be deceived.

How to start the game?

The admin can start the game. They have special controls in the lobby interface to begin the match when everyone is ready.
